Welcome to the dining room of **Spada Reale**, where each dish tells a story of our valleys.

The scent of wood and the warmth of the fireplace will guide you through a journey of authentic flavors and Piedmontese culinary traditions. Our dishes, prepared with local ingredients, vary with the seasons to ensure freshness and quality.

Whether it's an intimate dinner or a lunch with friends, the restaurant offers a welcoming atmosphere and attentive service. We look forward to letting you taste the true flavors of the mountains.
