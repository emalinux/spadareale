Lo Spada Reale offre diverse tipologie di camere per soddisfare le esigenze dei nostri ospiti:

Spada Reale offers different types of rooms to meet the needs of our guests:
• Small and cozy rooms, ideal for solo travelers.
• Double rooms.

All our rooms have a private internal bathroom, small but functional.

For longer stays, the Spada Reale holiday apartment is available, featuring two bedrooms, a bathroom, a spacious kitchen, and a living room.

Prices vary depending on the period and type of stay.
